Get access to internal documents

Already have an eRegulations job code? .

CMCS staff with policy-related responsibilities may request access to view and manage internal policy reference documents in eRegulations.

These internal documents are only available to CMCS staff who sign into eRegulations through CMS IDM. Before signing in for the first time, CMCS staff need to request and be approved for an EUA job code for eRegulations.

Request access for CMCS staff

  1. Sign in to the CMS EUA website.
  2. Click Modify My Job Codes.
  3. Read the screen text, check the Confirmation (Required) checkbox, and click Next.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the Modify My Job Codes: My Job Codes screen and click Add a Job Code.
  5. To find the job code, enter the following in the “Search for a group” box:
    1. Select Job Code from the first drop-down menu.
    2. Select = from the middle drop-down menu.
    3. Type or copy EREGS_EDITOR_PRD into the empty field.
    4. Click Search to the right of the job code field.
  6. Click the checkbox to confirm the job code, then click Select to move to the next screen.
  7. At this Modify My Job Codes: My Job Codes screen, do not click any checkboxes. Scroll to the bottom and click Next.
  8. At the Modify My Job Codes: My Job Codes screen:
    1. Enter a response in the Justification Reason field. Suggested language: I’m a CMCS staff member in [group and division name]. I am an eRegulations user and need access to internal policy repository documents for [reason].
    2. At the Is Job Code Temporary radio box, select No.
  9. Click Finish.
  10. Your job code request may take several days to process. When approved, you will receive an email with an update, and can then sign in to eRegulations. If you do not receive this email within 2 weeks, please contact Stephanie Boyd (stephanie.boyd@cms.hhs.gov).
